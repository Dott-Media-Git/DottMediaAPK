# Instagram Poster Template (bwinbet)

## Canvas
- Size: 1080x1350 (4:5)
- Background: Brand Gold (#FFCA08)

## Layout
- Header block: black rectangle across top-left 60% width, 18% height.
- Headline: 2-6 words, all caps, black or gold depending on block.
- Subhead: 1 short line under headline (stat, result, or moment).
- Accent: thin green line (#005540) under subhead.
- Body: player/action image cropped mid-frame; keep face and ball visible.
- Logo: bottom-left, width 220-260px, clear space = height of letter "b".

## Typography
- Headline: FreightSansProSemibold (fallback: FreightSans/Roboto), 88-120pt.
- Subhead: FreightSans/Roboto, 36-44pt.
- Optional stat badge: 28-32pt, black text on gold pill.

## Notes
- Keep text within safe zone: top 10% and bottom 12%.
- Use black text on gold, or gold text on black block.
