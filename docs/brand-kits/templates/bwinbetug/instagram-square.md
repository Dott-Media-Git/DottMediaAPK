# Instagram Square Template (bwinbet)

## Canvas
- Size: 1080x1080 (1:1)
- Background: Brand Gold (#FFCA08)

## Layout
- Headline block: black rectangle across top-left 55% width.
- Headline: 2-5 words, bold, all caps.
- Subhead: 1 short line with stat or match detail.
- Accent: thin green underline (#005540).
- Logo: bottom-left, width 200-240px, clear space = height of letter "b".

## Typography
- Headline: FreightSansProSemibold, 72-96pt.
- Subhead: Roboto, 28-36pt.

## Notes
- Keep text within central 70% width.
- Avoid clutter around the logo.
