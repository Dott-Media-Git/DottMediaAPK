# X Image Template (bwinbet)

## Canvas
- Size: 1600x900 (16:9)
- Background: Brand Green (#005540) or Black (#000000)

## Layout
- Headline block: black panel or gold strip across top-left 55% width.
- Headline: 2-5 words, bold, high contrast.
- Subhead: 1 line for context or scoreline.
- Accent: gold divider line or corner bracket.
- Logo: bottom-right or bottom-left, width 200-240px.

## Typography
- Headline: FreightSansProSemibold, 72-96pt.
- Subhead: Roboto, 28-36pt.

## Notes
- Keep text within central 70% width to avoid edge cropping.
- Use gold (#FFCA08) for key words or scoreline.
