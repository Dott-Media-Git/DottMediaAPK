# YouTube Thumbnail Template (bwinbet)

## Canvas
- Size: 1280x720 (16:9)
- Background: Dark green (#005540) or black with gold accents

## Layout
- Headline: 2-4 words, large, left aligned.
- Subhead: optional micro stat or opponent name.
- Accent: gold line or corner bracket.
- Logo: bottom-right, width 200-240px.

## Typography
- Headline: FreightSansProSemibold, 84-110pt.
- Subhead: Roboto, 28-36pt.

## Notes
- Keep text within center 70% width.
- Maximize contrast for small-screen visibility.
